Executive Summary

GoalThe electron–defect $T$-matrix spectral function beyond first Born, computed explicitly — EDI direct matrix elements + direct rest-space inversion, no Sternheimer equation.
Layer 1 — restBands 1–6 & 18–70 folded statically into $\tilde V(\omega_0)$ by one direct inverse $\mathcal G^Q=[N_k(\omega_0-H_0^Q)-V_{QQ}]^{-1}$ (full-order in $V_{QQ}$; $1/N_k$ BZ measure).
Layer 2 — activeBands 7–17 resummed exactly: $T_{PP}(\omega)=[1-\tilde V G^A(\omega)]^{-1}\tilde V$; spectral function $A(\omega)=-\tfrac1\pi\,\mathrm{Im\,Tr}[G^A+G^A T_{PP}G^A]$.
OutcomeThe S-vacancy in-gap levels — an $a_1$ state near VBM and an $e$-doublet at $+1.19$–$1.21$ eV — agree across two pseudopotentials, direct diagonalization, the $T$-matrix, Anvil's independent EDT code, and supercell DFT ($+1.17$). Matrix elements are Hermitian ($\max|M-M^\dagger|=0$).

Method at a Glance

Everything downstream of the DFT inputs is post-processing of the EDI direct matrix elements:

$$\langle\psi_{nk}|\Delta V|\psi_{mk'}\rangle\;\xrightarrow{\text{partition }A/R}\;\mathcal G^Q=[N_k(\omega_0-H_0^Q)-V_{QQ}]^{-1}\;\xrightarrow{\text{downfold}}\;\tilde V=V_{PP}+V_{PQ}\mathcal G^Q V_{QP}\;\xrightarrow{\text{resum}}\;T_{PP}(\omega)=[1-\tilde V G^A(\omega)]^{-1}\tilde V$$

The construction is the explicit-summation counterpart of the $k$-decoupled Sternheimer ladder: because the rest space here is finite and enumerable (bands 1–6, 18–70 on the 12×12 grid), $\mathcal G^Q$ is obtained by one direct $8496\times8496$ inversion rather than iterative solves — full-order in $V_{QQ}$ by construction.
